Porting CESM to Shaheen DocumentationÂ¶

This documents our attempt to port the Community Earth System Model (CESM) to Shaheen II which is a Cray XC40 at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology.

The purpose of porting CESM is to test data assimilation algorithms using 1000-member ensembles of the Community Atmosphere Model (CAM).
